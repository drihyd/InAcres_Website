Social Responsibility

Over the years, we have successfully grown into a fully-equipped and able agricultural enterprise that is eager to create a positive social change in the environment through organic and responsible farming practices.

We engage in sustainable agriculture on an integrated and turnkey basis. We work closely with nurseries and practise the urban-friendly model of Community-Supported Agriculture. We also support rural and tribal food growers with our know-how and have included the organic produce in our spectrum of services. This creates a rewarding future for both the farming community as well as the environment

We train rural producers on sustainable and integrated farming procedures as a socially-oriented service. Moreover, through SAATTVIC, we voice our opinion for sustainability and offer organic produce to the consumers. SriSti Trust, a positive health initiative started by us encourages holistic living.
