At InAcres, a piece of farmland is much more than that

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) originated in Japan in 1971 with an aim to cultivate healthfulness through sustainable, organic & pesticide-free food. Through a direct connection between the consumers and the food growers, CSA paves the way to a safer and healthier food production system.

We harmoniously combine the strengths of Community-Supported Agriculture and the sustainability of agroforestry to create a greener future. Simply put, such a model allows the food growers to employ traditional methods and modern techniques, aided by the monetary support of the consumer community — thereby sharing both the risks and rewards.

Here’s how our CSA model works

Our CSA model includes scientifically laid out plots backed by expert-decided cropping plan to create a successful 50-50 sharing plan between the consumers and the food growers. This fosters efficiency and value addition across the entire farm.

As you decide to begin a sustainable journey with us, we help you get on board with the farming model all the while managing and maintaining your farmland with the help of our team.

Engage with a like-minded and passionate community with direct access to organic produce from both Shadnagar and Bhuthpur CSA farms.

Certified authentically organic by India Organic

We follow Vrikshayurveda, the farm practices written by Maharshi Surapala . They enable us to cultivate produce naturally as well as enhancing its mineral and nutrient content. By sustaining the quality of soils through the comprehensive study of ‘arbori-horticulture’, we have been able to streamline the growth of plants and other farming practices.
