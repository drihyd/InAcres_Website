Bhutpur CSA

After having successfully executed a role model Akshaya CSA farm at Shadnagar, we have now identified a new project near Salikalapur village, Khilla Ghanpur mandal, Wanaparthy district.

Project Highlights

  • Situated 90 km from the Shamshabad exit on the Hyderabad ORR.
  • Secured and well-connected perimeter
  • 6-8 months of water supply in a year from the Kalwakurthy Lift Irrigation Canal.
  • Karivena Reservoir Project which is currently under construction is visible from the site.
  • Home to a 7-year old mango plantation.
  • Irrigated by 4 functioning bore-wells, a water storage tank and drip irrigation system.
  • Authentically organic farming methods and produce.
  • Sandalwood plantation is also planned across the site.
