Our Story

A passion for farming and love towards sustainability are rooted deep within the story of InAcres. Over the years, we observed the conventional practices employed in agriculture. Eventually, we noticed that, due to an ever-increasing food demand across the country, they required an extensive dosage of synthetic pesticides and fertilisers; external energy and high labour inputs.

It became evident that such methods and practices packed various economic, environmental and public health challenges which required an immediate resolution. One that was both sustainable and eco-friendly.

And this engendered the CSA model at InAcres


The history

Our journey began in the year 2000 at a nursery in Shimoga, Karnataka. And over the last 15 years, we were able to successfully grow into a fully-equipped agricultural enterprise.

With the help of a like-minded team and community, we nurtured our expertise and set up multiple Community-Supported Agriculture farms, turnkey sustainable organic services and food courts.
